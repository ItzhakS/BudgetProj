Just Budge It...

This is your new Motto. The path to succesful financial autonomy is *drum roll please* Budgeting. That's right, away with your wanton spending. All your hard-earned income should be directed carefully towards specific expenses. If your expenses out-weigh your income, you need to cut back and spend less... Or get a higher paying job.

In the next window enter your different income sources and their corresponding amounts. Then enter your expenses. Lastly you will find the difference between your income and expenses, as well as the main sources of income and expense.
